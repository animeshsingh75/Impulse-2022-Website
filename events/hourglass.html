HOUR GLASS

Are you a chess player? Time to put your skills to test. Participate in the Chess Competion in Impluse and prove that you are the best at your game!

Venue : Maharaja Agrasen Institute Of Technology

Date and Time : 12-13 May

Registration Deadline : 8 May 11:59 PM

Rules

This is a offline chess competition.

1. Participants must be at the venue on time. Latecomers will not be allowed to participate.

2. Chess board will be provided on the venue.

3. It will be a timed game, i.e. there will be a time limit for each player.

4. Castling is allowed in the game.

5. You have to move the piece you touch.

6. in case of stalemate, a rematch will be done.

7. Cheating of any kind will not be tolerated at all.

FAQ's

Do the participants need to bring their chess board?

Yes, it will be prefered to bring a chess board.

What would happen in case of a stalemate?

A rematch will be done in case of stalemate.
